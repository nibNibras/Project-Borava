# 🗺️ BORAVA: The Soul of Java (Yogyakarta Exploration Platform)
A beautiful, responsive culture and tourism landing page dedicated to showcasing the rich heritage, culinary wonders, and hidden gems of Yogyakarta. Built using native front-end web technologies, this platform serves as an interactive travel guide featuring historical storytelling, a multi-destination showcase, traditional food spotlights, and deep cultural insights.

# 🌐 Website Sections
The platform guides users through an immersive journey across four core sections:

- <strong>🏠 Home (Hero):</strong> Captivates visitors with inspiring taglines ("Heritage, hotspots, and hidden gems found in one unforgettable escape"), inviting them to explore the rich stories of Yogyakarta via the "Explore More" call-to-action button.

- <strong>📜 History:</strong> A narrative block capturing the essence of Jogja—from its roots in the Islamic Mataram Kingdom in 1755 and its crucial role as Indonesia's revolutionary capital (1946–1949), to its modern identity as the city of education and culture.

- <strong>💎 Hidden Gems (Destination Grid):</strong> A highly organized, 10-spot interactive grid showcasing Yogyakarta’s most iconic historical sites, nature treks, and art festivals:

  - <strong>Heritage & Culture:</strong> Candi Borobudur, Candi Prambanan, Keraton Yogyakarta, Taman Sari.

  - <strong>Nature & Adventure:</strong> Gunung Merapi, Pantai Parangtritis, Goa Pindul, Hutan Pinus Mangunan, Kaliurang.

  - <strong>Modern Art:</strong> Artjog.

- <strong>🍲 Taste of Tradition (Food):</strong> A curated culinary map highlighting authentic flavors that define the local identity, including Gudeg, Tongseng Kambing, Bakpia Pathok, Mangut Lele, Yangko, and Sate Klatak.

- <strong>👣 Footer (BORAVA):</strong> A clean summary area providing navigation shortcuts and reinforcing the branding tagline: “Discovering the history, culture, and beauty of Jogja in one unforgettable trip.”
